ABOUT US
The iCast has been implemented to transcend the limitations of traditional voting methods, ushering in a new era of student council elections. Tailored for PUP's diverse departments, this system ensures that every student's voice is not only heard but accurately represented.

iCast embodies a transformative shift towards enhanced efficiency, inclusivity, and transparency in voting systems. Through its streamlined interface, robust security measures, and centralized database, iCast paves the way for a new era where casting votes becomes not only convenient but also empowered by the force of innovation.
